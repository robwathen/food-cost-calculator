How the math works

Each ingredient: Purchase Unit Cost ÷ Portions per Unit = cost per portion, then × Portion Qty = extended cost. The build cost is the sum of every ingredient's extended cost. "Portions per Unit" should reflect real usable yield after trim and waste — for sauces and spreads, count usable ounces per jar. Food cost % bands: ≤ 32% good  ·  32–38% watch  ·  > 38% over target.

Note for the PimCore build: the three locked columns (ingredient, portion unit, portion qty) would be pulled live from the menu item's recipe. The franchisee's three entries (purchase unit, cost, portions per unit) would be saved per franchisee so they can return to their numbers later.
